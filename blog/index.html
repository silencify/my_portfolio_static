Trekking to Tilicho

It was cold, it was exhausting, it was miserable but every moment of the trip was exciting. This is about my experience of trekking to Tilicho. Me and my friends - Bishal, Bibek - out of the blue decided to go trekking to Tilicho, partially planned. We did some findings on the itinerary and planned how to reach tilicho. We took the bus from Kathmandu to Besishahar and from there took a jeep to Chame.
